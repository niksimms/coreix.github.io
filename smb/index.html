At Coreix, we offer flexible, scalable colocation, managed hosting and network solutions to match your business requirements.

Hosting your infrastructure offsite

Hosting your equipment off site offers a reassurance that your servers, systems and infrastructure are being securely protected within the confines of an industry accredited datacentre on a 24x7x365 basis by an experienced team of datacentre and network engineers. As standard, we have robust physical and network security policies, procedures and processes in place to ensure that your infrastructure is hosted in the most optimal hosting environment. Your online business is our priority and we ensure that your systems are operational on a 24x7x365.

Robust and secure tier III datacentre facilities to host your infrastructure

Whether you need a quarter rack or a private suite, you will have the option to scale up or down service requirements as and when you need to. Each rack is powered by A+B feeds as standard with 2 N+N UPS protection as well as N+1 power redundancy giving you complete protection. Coreix offers complete network redundancy with connections to an array of tier 1 and tier 2 carriers and internet exchange points via its resilient metro ring.

Agility and flexibility to scale your cloud requirements

Our public cloud service offers the ideal solution for SMBs looking for a scalable pay as you go hosting solution. With our easy to manage app, free firewall service, free bandwidth upload backed up by a 24x7 support team and choice of operating system, businesses can have peace of mind that their organisation will never suffer from a moment of downtime and be able to monitor and adjust their cloud storage as and when they need to.

Dedicated server solutions onsite designed for quick deployment

Coreix stocks an array of Dell servers ranging from a single 1u server with 16GB ram to multi-site solutions so that we can cater to your needs 24x7x365. All servers can be ordered directly and be quickly online within a four hour period – ideal for urgent requirements. If you don't see what you need here, please contact our sales team who will be happy to provide you with a quote from our full dedicated server range.

Whether you require a server upgrade, additional storage, or need to install a firewall, our experienced technical services team are available on a 24x7x365 basis to guide you through which dedicated server solution will suit your IT strategy and budget.

We will work with you to deploy your hardware solutions and ensure that your server is online within a four hours so that your business can concentrate what it does best, while we focus on the backend ensure that your business doesn’t experience downtime.

Responsive response team

As standard, we will answer your email within a five minute period and will tailor a solution which suits your requirements. We offer proactive monitoring and will notify you in real time if our systems pick up any issues with your equipment. We also allow you to define an escalation procedure so if instructed to, we can immediately start work on your server if there is a problem.

Each member of our team will offer advice on backup and restoration, database optimisation, hack recovery, security auditing, virtualisation technologies as well as web hosting technologies and control panels.
