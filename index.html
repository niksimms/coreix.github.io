Who we are

Coreix specialises in providing high availability hosting services and solutions for small, medium and large enterprise business. Coreix was formed in 2003 to answer the need for a customer-driven managed hosting provider with unparalleled expertise, cutting-edge technology and award winning industry-leading customer service.

From our securely operated facilities in London, UK we are able to provide services ranging from a single dedicated server to managed multi-server clusters, secure cages or private suites complimented by 24/7 onsite staff and premium network bandwidth or point-to-point connections to the carrier of your choice.

With over 30 years of managed dedicated hosting experience between our management we are in a unique position to stand out from the crowd. Our company is driven by our passion and dedication to our customers needs.

By offering the most comprehensive hosting solutions in the market place we are able to offer you a solution that is individually tailored to your companies needs now and in the future. Our aggressive pricing and managed services combine to allow you to take advantage of the economies of scale regardless of the size of your solution.
