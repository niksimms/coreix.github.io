Experienced responsive operations team

Our experienced and qualified front line technical support team, datacentre, operations and network connectivity team are available 24x7x365 and are trained in all aspects of managing the day to day operations of the colocation facilities with qualifications ranging from MCSE to CCIE and RHCE. The technical support team are able to offer advice on backup and restoration, database optimisation, hack recovery, security auditing, virtualisation technologies as well as web hosting technologies and control panels.

Further to this, the company’s network engineering team monitor and manage the network from the network operations centre which is situated within our secure, robust tier III facility in Enfield. All engineers at CCIE are trained to at least CCIE (Cisco) or JNCIE (Juniper) standards and are able to advise and assist with any client requirement to include clustering, emergency diagnostics, firewalls, load balancing, routing and switching and topology design and implementation.
